What is The Percentage In Mathematics?

In mathematics, a percentage is defined as a quantity or ratio that represents a fraction of 100. It is commonly denoted by the symbol "%" or, less commonly, as "percent" and "pct." For example, when computing percentages, 35% is comparable to the decimal 0.35 and is written as 35 / 100 in fraction form.

Percent vs. Percentage:

Whenever the term % appears, it is generally used in conjunction with a number. Take, for example, 50%. However, the word % is rarely used in conjunction with a number. For example, how much of the color box is red?
